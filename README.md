# Overview
Company wants to analyzing the sales data of an online retail store to identify the key factors contributing to revenue. The goal was to generate useful insights that could help the CEO and CMO understand business performance from both operational and marketing perspectives.

We focused on identifying important trends, high-performing areas, and customer demographics that influence sales. The insights generated can help the business make better strategic decisions and plan future growth.


# Problem Statement & Objective
- Identify top revenue-generating products, countries, and customer segments.

- Provide insights to help the CEO and CMO make better operational and marketing decisions.

- Analyze sales trends and patterns to support future business planning.

- Highlight high-performing areas to guide the company’s expansion strategy.
